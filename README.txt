Controls
--------

A - Move Left
D - Move Right
W/Spacebar/UpArrow - Jump (Tap in the air to Double Jump)
F/LMouseBtn - Fire Projectile

Gamepad Controls also work from the getgo.


Jump into the puddle to absorb water.
Double Jump to inhale air.
Use the Helium Tank to inhale Helium.


Get to the end of the level!
